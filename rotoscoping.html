1-Minute Video Rotoscope

A technical deep-dive into frame-by-frame rotoscoping, demonstrating precision masking and subject isolation techniques.

The Rotoscoping Pipeline

01. Selection & Prep

Choosing footage with high contrast between subject and background is crucial. A clean source file ensures the mask anchors snap correctly during the frame-by-frame process.

02. Bezier Pathing

Using the pen tool to create fluid Bezier curves. I focus on key anatomical points to ensure the mask follows the subject's silhouette even during high-motion frames.
